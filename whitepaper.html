A 24/7 market for tokenized sports cards, powered by Solana and a live performance oracle.

Executive Summary

Sports Pay Coin (SPC) transforms physical sports cards into dynamic, tradable assets through fractional ownership, dual-token utility, and real-time performance pricing. The result: liquidity for collectors, access for fans, and a 24/7 market that reacts to every game, every play, every moment.

This whitepaper covers the tokenization workflow, the Sports Oracle that powers dynamic pricing, our token economics, presale structure, and the roadmap to a live exchange.

The Problem

📦

Illiquid Collectibles

High-value sports cards take weeks or months to sell. Auction cycles, escrow, shipping, insurance — friction at every step. Capital is locked.

🚪

Locked-Out Fans

A $6M Babe Ruth or a million-dollar LeBron rookie is unreachable for the everyday fan. The collector market has become a club for the wealthy.

🎭

Counterfeit Risk

Fakes, "cleaned" graded cards, and swap scams plague the market. There is no immutable, public history of who owned what, and when.

Our Solution

🔒

Vault & Mint

Cards are submitted to partnered vaults, certified, imaged, and minted on-chain as immutable digital twins. The card never leaves the vault.

📈

Tokenized Trading

Each tokenized card lists on the SPC exchange. Buy whole, or buy fractional shares for as little as $3. 24/7 trading, instant settlement.

📡

Sports Oracle

A trusted data feed pipes scores, stats, injuries, sentiment, and odds onto the chain. Token prices react to live performance.

In short

  • From physical card to digital asset: three steps (vault → mint → list)
  • Fractional shares democratize access to elite collectibles
  • The oracle makes every game a market event
  • Immutable provenance ends the counterfeit problem

The Tokenization Workflow

Step 1: Asset Initiation & Vaulting

The user submits their high-value sports memorabilia or card to one of our specialized, partnered vault facilities. These established professional vaults ensure physical security and authenticity from the moment of receipt.

Step 2: Certification & Digital Minting

The vault partner conducts rigorous certification — high-resolution imaging, physical authentication, grading verification. Once certified, the asset's unique data is minted as a tokenized asset on the Sports Pay Coin blockchain, creating an immutable digital twin that serves as proof of ownership and provenance.

Step 3: Liquidity & Exchange Listing

The newly tokenized asset goes live on our proprietary exchange. Users can buy, sell, or trade the asset instantly using Sports Pay Coin as the native currency — unlocking liquidity previously impossible with physical collectibles.

The Sports Oracle

The Sports Oracle is a trusted data feed that brings real-world sports information on-chain — final scores, player statistics, injury reports, social sentiment, and betting odds. This data feeds smart contracts that automatically adjust token prices based on predefined rules.

Without an oracle: your token price moves like a museum painting — only when someone buys or sells. With an oracle: your token price moves like a public stock — reacting instantly to games, news, and fan sentiment.

Oracle Capabilities

  • Fantasy-meets-investing: 5-touchdown game increases token value automatically
  • Real-time engagement: trade on injuries, buzzer-beaters, record performances
  • Automatic buy walls: smart contracts support prices during positive news
  • Social sentiment integration: Twitter buzz, fan polls, odds movement
  • Permissionless ecosystem: other developers can build on the oracle data
  • Reduced manipulation: rate-limited withdrawals and dispute mechanisms
  • Backtesting capability: historical data validates pricing models before launch
