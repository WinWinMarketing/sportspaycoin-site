The Workflow

From card to cash, in three steps.

Vault → Mint → Trade. Your physical sports card becomes a tokenized asset on Solana — fractional, liquid, and tradable 24/7.

The Process

Three steps. Zero friction.

No shipping, no insurance, no auctions. Your card stays in a vault — its digital twin trades on the open market.

Step 1

Asset initiation & vaulting

Submit your high-value sports memorabilia or card to one of our partnered vault facilities — professional, insured, and already trusted by the collectibles industry. Physical security and authenticity from the moment of receipt.

  • Fort Knox-grade physical security
  • Climate-controlled, fully insured
  • No shipping risk, no handling, no exposure
Step 2

Certification & digital minting

The vault partner runs a rigorous certification process — high-resolution imaging, physical authentication, grading verification. Your card's unique data is then minted as a tokenized asset on the Sports Pay Coin blockchain. An immutable digital twin, with provenance baked in.

  • High-res imaging and grading verification
  • Immutable proof of ownership on-chain
  • Every transaction recorded forever
Step 3

Liquidity & exchange listing

Your tokenized asset goes live on the proprietary Sports Pay Coin exchange. Anyone can buy a fractional share — as little as $3 of a card worth millions — using SPC as the native currency. Instant settlement. No auctions. No waiting.

  • List in minutes, not months
  • Fractional shares — open to every fan
  • 24/7 trading, instant settlement
Why Tokenize

A static collectible becomes a dynamic financial instrument.

Eight benefits of tokenizing real-world sports assets.

Fractional Ownership

Split a million-dollar card into thousands of $3 shares. Democratizes access to elite collectibles.

24/7 Liquidity

Trade instantly instead of waiting weeks or months for a physical auction. The market never closes.

DeFi Integration
